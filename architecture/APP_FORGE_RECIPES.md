# AppForge Product Recipes

Build baseline: 1.01.1  
Recipe schema: 1

Product Recipes are validated starting definitions for creating distinct apps from the shared Field Vault core. They prove configuration portability without switching the active FireVault app or touching its customer vault.

## Registered recipes

| Recipe | Category | Blueprint | Publication | Required before publication |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| FireVault | Field service | 9/9 ready | Active | None |
| Wyoming Explorer | Travel guide | 9/9 ready | Foundation | Brand icons and verified Wyoming database |
| Wyoming Fishing Guide | Outdoor guide | 9/9 ready | Foundation | Brand icons, verified locations, current regulations |
| Ghost Towns Guide | Themed travel | 9/9 ready | Foundation | Brand icons and verified historic-sites database |

## What changes per recipe

- App identity, audience, purpose, terminology, navigation, wordmark, tagline, and colors
- Enabled shared modules and action surfaces
- Record fields, detail sections, and photo categories
- Selected data sources and content packs
- Offline, backup, provider, and collaboration policy

## Safety boundary

Recipes are downloadable JSON build inputs and executable Generator Engine inputs. Downloading or packaging one does not activate it, change FireVault labels, modify the installed PWA, migrate storage, or expose customer data. Publication readiness is separate from blueprint and package validity.
